# Social Media Scripts — Short-Form Video

> 10 scripts for TikTok, Facebook Reels, and LinkedIn. Each follows the Hook → Problem → Agitate → Solution → CTA framework. Keep every video under 60 seconds.

---

"And here's the worst part — you don't own any of it. Stop paying? You lose access to your client list, your job history, your invoices. Everything. You're renting your own business data."

[B-ROLL SUGGESTION: Close-up of someone clicking "Cancel Subscription" and seeing a warning about data loss]

"What if I told you that you could own all of that — the scheduling, the invoicing, the CRM, even an AI receptionist — for less than what you pay in a single month of subscriptions? One-time cost. You own the code. No monthly fees. Ever."

[B-ROLL SUGGESTION: Split screen — left side shows monthly bills stacking up, right side shows a clean custom dashboard]

"We're doing free 15-minute software audits for contractors right now. Link in bio. Let's see how much you're actually spending and what you could save."

[B-ROLL SUGGESTION: Phone screen showing the booking link]

## Posting Guidelines

### Platform-Specific Notes

| Platform | Best Length | Format | Best Times |
|----------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| TikTok | 30-45 sec | Vertical 9:16, face-to-camera + B-roll | 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-10 PM |
| Facebook Reels | 30-60 sec | Vertical 9:16, captions mandatory | 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM |
| LinkedIn | 45-60 sec | Vertical or square, more professional tone | 7-9 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM Tue-Thu |
| Instagram Reels | 30-45 sec | Vertical 9:16, trending audio optional | 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM |
| YouTube Shorts | 30-55 sec | Vertical 9:16, strong hook | 12-3 PM, 7-10 PM |

### General Tips

1. **First 3 seconds are everything.** If you don't hook them, they scroll.
2. **Add captions to every video.** 80%+ of people watch without sound.
3. **Face-to-camera is king.** People connect with faces, not graphics.
4. **Post consistently.** 1 video/day is ideal. Minimum 3/week.
5. **Repurpose across all platforms.** One video = 5 posts (TikTok, FB, IG, LinkedIn, YT Shorts).
6. **Use a strong CTA.** Always tell them what to do next.
7. **Pin your best-performing video.** New visitors see it first.
8. **Respond to every comment.** The algorithm rewards engagement.
