# Follow-Up Sequences

> Email and text follow-up sequences for every stage of the sales pipeline. Copy, customize, and send.

---

## Sequence 1: After Advice Call Booking (Confirmation)

**Trigger:** Prospect books a strategy call via Atlas or booking link.

---

### Immediate — Confirmation Text (SMS)

> Hey [Name]! This is [Your Name] from Tektora AI. You're booked for [Day] at [Time]. I'm going to audit your software stack and show you how other contractors are saving thousands. Talk soon!

---

### Immediate — Confirmation Email

**Subject:** You're booked — here's what to expect on [Day]

> Hey [Name],
>
> Thanks for booking a call with us. Here's what to expect:
>
> **When:** [Day, Date] at [Time]
> **Duration:** 15 minutes (I'll keep it tight)
> **How:** [Zoom link / Phone — I'll call you at (XXX) XXX-XXXX]
>
> **What we'll cover:**
> - A quick look at the tools you're currently using to run your business
> - What other contractors in your space are doing differently
> - Whether there's a way to cut your software costs by 60-80%
>
> **What to have ready (optional but helpful):**
> - A rough idea of what software you're paying for monthly
> - Any frustrations you have with your current tools
>
> No pitch, no pressure. If there's a fit, I'll let you know. If not, you'll still walk away with some useful info.
>
> Talk soon,
> [Your Name]
> Tektora AI

---

### Day Before — Reminder Text (SMS)

> Hey [Name], just a quick reminder about our call tomorrow at [Time]. Looking forward to it! If anything came up and you need to reschedule, just let me know.

---

### 1 Hour Before — Reminder Text (SMS)

> Hey [Name], we're on in about an hour. I'll [call you / send the Zoom link] at [Time]. Talk soon!

## Sequence 2: After Strategy Call — No Close

**Trigger:** Strategy call completed, prospect didn't commit to a proposal or said "not right now."

---

### Day 1 — Thank You Text (SMS)

> Hey [Name], thanks for hopping on the call today. Good stuff. I'll shoot you over some info on what we talked about. No rush on anything — just want you to have it.

---

### Day 1 — Thank You Email

**Subject:** Good chatting today, [Name]

> Hey [Name],
>
> Thanks for taking the time today. It was great learning about [Business Name] and how you're running things.
>
> Here's a quick recap:
> - You're currently spending roughly **$[X]/month** on software ($[X x 12]/year)
> - The tools you mentioned: [Tool 1, Tool 2, Tool 3]
> - Biggest frustration: [What they said]
>
> For reference, we build custom AI tools that replace all of that for a one-time investment. You own the code, no monthly fees, no lock-in.
>
> No pressure at all. If you ever want to see what that would look like for your business, I'm a text away.
>
> [Your Name]
> Tektora AI

---

### Day 3 — Value Add Text (SMS)

> Hey [Name], random thought — I was talking to another [plumber/electrician/contractor] today and they were paying $[X]/month for [Tool]. Sound familiar? We replaced it with a custom tool for a one-time cost. Just thought of you.

---

### Day 7 — Email with Social Proof

**Subject:** Quick update + a story you might relate to

> Hey [Name],
>
> Quick story — we just wrapped up a build for a [similar trade] contractor who was paying $1,100/month across Jobber, QuickBooks, and a call answering service.
>
> We built him a custom suite that does everything those tools did — plus an AI receptionist that answers his phone 24/7. Total investment: $5,000. One time.
>
> He's saving over $13,000/year. And he owns everything.
>
> I keep thinking about our conversation and I'm pretty sure we could do something similar for [Business Name]. Want me to put together a quick proposal? No obligation.
>
> [Your Name]

---

### Day 14 — Final Check-In Text (SMS)

> Hey [Name], it's [Your Name] from Tektora AI. Just circling back one more time. Still happy to put together a free proposal showing what your custom tool build would look like. After this I'll get out of your hair — just didn't want you to miss out. Let me know either way!

---

### Day 14 — Final Check-In Email

**Subject:** Last follow-up from me

> Hey [Name],
>
> I don't want to be that guy, so this will be my last follow-up.
>
> If you're ever interested in seeing what it would look like to replace your software stack with tools you actually own, the offer's always open. No expiry.
>
> Wishing you and [Business Name] the best. Keep crushing it.
>
> [Your Name]
> Tektora AI
>
> P.S. If the timing's just off and you'd like me to check back in a few months, just reply "check back" and I'll set a reminder.
